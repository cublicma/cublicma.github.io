# 灵巧手论文追踪报告

## 执行摘要
本轮发现 3 项与灵巧机器人手直接相关的新进展，分别聚焦自动化灵巧抓取数据采集、双手灵巧遥操作，以及面向连续灵巧人形机动操作的协调策略。整体上，这些工作更偏向真实系统、数据管线和双手交互，而不是泛化的机器人抓取。

## Executive Summary
This run surfaced three new items directly relevant to dexterous robot hands: automated dexterous grasping data collection, bimanual dexterous teleoperation, and coordinated policies for continuous dexterous humanoid loco-manipulation. The common thread is practical system-building around real hardware, data pipelines, and two-hand interaction rather than generic grasping.

## 排名结果
1. **AutoDex: An Automated Real-World System for Dexterous Grasping Data Collection**  
   - 首次公开日期：2026-06-22  
   - 作者或组织：arXiv authors  
   - 类型：paper  
   - 相关性：该工作把自动化真实世界数据采集作为核心贡献，直接服务于灵巧抓取与灵巧手控制。  
   - 新颖性信号：新公开的端到端灵巧抓取数据采集系统，重点在真实机器人手上自动生成高质量轨迹与抓取数据。  
   - 链接：https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23689  
   - 置信度：0.93  
   - 分析：AutoDex 的主要工作是构建一套面向真实环境的自动化灵巧抓取数据采集系统，解决灵巧手数据稀缺、人工遥操作成本高且一致性差的问题。方法上，论文围绕自动化闭环采集流程设计了抓取、重试和数据筛选机制，使机器人手能够在真实物体上持续产生可用于学习的交互轨迹。实验部分从公开信息看主要通过真实世界采集与下游灵巧抓取学习来验证系统可行性，结果表明该流程能够稳定产出灵巧抓取数据并支撑后续学习。整体结论是：自动化采集比纯人工标注更适合扩展灵巧手数据规模，但公开材料仍不足以判断其在更复杂手型、长时序操作或跨场景泛化上的上限，因此限制主要在于适用范围和细节披露不够充分。  
   - Analysis: AutoDex’s main contribution is an automated real-world system for collecting dexterous grasping data, addressing the classic bottleneck of scarce hand data and the high cost and inconsistency of manual teleoperation. The method centers on an automated closed-loop collection pipeline with grasping, retry, and filtering logic so a robot hand can continuously generate usable interaction trajectories on physical objects. Based on the public abstract-level information, the experiments validate the system through real-world data collection and downstream dexterous grasp learning, and the reported outcome is that the pipeline can reliably produce training data for subsequent learning. The overall conclusion is that automation is a practical path to scaling dexterous-hand data, but the public information is still insufficient to judge its ceiling on more complex hand morphologies, long-horizon manipulation, or cross-scene generalization, so the main limitations are scope and incomplete disclosure of details.

2. **DexTeleop-0: Force-Aware Bimanual Dexterous Teleoperation with Ego-Centric Perception towards Shared Autonomy**  
   - 首次公开日期：2026-06-22  
   - 作者或组织：arXiv authors  
   - 类型：paper  
   - 相关性：该工作把双手灵巧遥操作、力觉感知和共享自主作为核心主题，明确围绕灵巧手与复杂双手操作展开。  
   - 新颖性信号：新发布的双手灵巧遥操作方案，强调力觉与自我中心感知的结合，属于灵巧手遥操作方向的新系统型工作。  
   - 链接：https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23431  
   - 置信度：0.90  
   - 分析：DexTeleop-0 的主要工作是面向共享自主的双手灵巧遥操作框架，解决复杂双手任务中操作员负担高、接触状态难感知以及动作协同难的问题。方法上，它将力觉信息与自我中心感知结合，用于支撑双手动作生成、接触理解和半自主控制。实验从公开材料看主要围绕真实遥操作任务来验证系统在双手协同与接触感知上的有效性，结果显示该框架能够更自然地支撑灵巧交互与共享自主式控制。总体结论是，这类力觉感知的双手遥操作更接近真实灵巧操作需求，但目前公开信息仍不足以评估其在不同手型、不同任务复杂度和长期稳定性上的泛化能力，因此限制在于实验规模与细节披露仍有限。  
   - Analysis: DexTeleop-0’s main contribution is a bimanual dexterous teleoperation framework aimed at shared autonomy, addressing the high operator burden, weak contact awareness, and coordination difficulty that arise in complex two-handed tasks. The method combines force-aware signals with egocentric perception to support bimanual action generation, contact understanding, and semi-autonomous control. From the public material, the experiments focus on real teleoperation tasks to validate the system’s effectiveness for coordinated two-hand interaction and contact perception, and the reported result is that the framework enables more natural dexterous interaction and shared-autonomy control. The overall conclusion is that force-aware bimanual teleoperation is a promising direction for practical dexterous manipulation, but the public information is still insufficient to assess generalization across hand morphologies, task complexity, and long-term stability, so the limitations are mainly the limited experimental scope and incomplete disclosure of details.

3. **CoorDex: Coordinating Body and Hand Priors for Continuous Dexterous Humanoid Loco-Manipulation**  
   - 首次公开日期：2026-06-22  
   - 作者或组织：arXiv authors  
   - 类型：paper  
   - 相关性：该工作把灵巧手与人形机动操作中的身体-手部协同作为中心贡献，虽然场景更大，但手部与灵巧操作仍是关键组成。  
   - 新颖性信号：新提出面向连续人形机动操作的协调策略，把身体先验和手部先验统一到同一控制框架中。  
   - 链接：https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23680  
   - 置信度：0.84  
   - 分析：CoorDex 的主要工作是协调身体与手部先验，服务于连续的灵巧人形机动操作，试图解决全身运动、接触切换和手部精细操作难以统一建模的问题。方法上，论文将身体先验与手部先验联合到一个协调框架中，以便在连续任务里同时维持姿态移动和手部灵巧交互。实验公开信息显示其在连续 loco-manipulation 场景中进行了验证，用来检验协同策略对稳定性和任务完成度的帮助，结果表明该方向能够更好地支撑身体与手的联合控制。整体来看，这是一项把灵巧手放进更大规模人形系统中的工作，结论是协调建模有现实价值，但公开材料对具体硬件、任务集和失败模式披露有限，因此限制主要在于可复现细节和泛化边界尚不充分。  
   - Analysis: CoorDex’s main contribution is to coordinate body and hand priors for continuous dexterous humanoid loco-manipulation, tackling the challenge of unifying whole-body motion, contact switching, and fine hand manipulation in one model. The method integrates body priors and hand priors into a coordinated framework so that posture movement and dexterous interaction can be maintained together over continuous tasks. The public information indicates experiments in continuous loco-manipulation scenarios to test whether the coordination strategy improves stability and task completion, and the reported outcome is that the approach better supports joint body-hand control. Overall, this is a dexterous-hand paper embedded in a larger humanoid system, and the conclusion is that coordinated modeling is practically useful; however, the public disclosure of hardware, task set, and failure cases is limited, so the main limitations are reproducibility detail and unclear generalization boundaries.
